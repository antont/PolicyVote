## Contact

### Visit us physically or virtually

We host impactful events both in our centre and online for government institutions, companies, and media. You’ll get an overview of e-Estonia’s best practices and build links to leading IT-service providers and state experts to support your digitalisation plans.

[Book here](https://booking.e-estonia.com/)

### Questions? Have a chat with us.

E-mail: [e-estonia@eis.ee](mailto:e-estonia@eis.ee)

Media: [press.e-estonia@eis.ee](mailto:press.e-estonia@eis.ee)

Call us: [+372 6273157](tel:+372 6273157) (Monday to Friday, 9:00-16:30 Estonian time)

Regarding e-Residency, visit their [official webpage](https://www.e-resident.gov.ee/).

### Find us

The Briefing Centre is conveniently located just a 2-minute drive from the airport and around 10- to 15-minute drive from the city centre.

You will find us on the ground floor of Valukoja 8, at the [central entrance](https://e-estonia.com/wp-content/uploads/esitluskeskus-1-scaled.jpg) behind the statue of Mr Ernst Julius Öpik. We will meet the delegation at the building’s reception. Kindly note that a booking is required to visit us.
